Smoked Queso

Smoked Queso is a big hit at any party!

When you need an appetizer or snack that is sure to be a crowd pleaser at your next Super Bowl party or BBQ, Smoked Queso is an excellent choice that is a winner every time. It's simple to make and packed with bold flavors. And the best part? You can swap out different cheeses or meats (or even go meatless) to make this recipe your own and come up with all sorts of great flavor combinations. All you need to make this recipe is a smoker (or grill and add a smoker box) and a few hours to let it soak up the great smoky taste and have all the flavors melt together. Here is how to make this simple yet tasty recipe:

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 2-3 hours

    Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground sausage
  • 1 lb Velveeta
  • 16 oz Cream Cheese
